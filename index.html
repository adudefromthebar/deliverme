WHO ARE YOU, AND WHY'D YOU DO IT?

DeliverMe is made by 3 young entrepreneurs: Christopher Ng, Andrew Ganni and Nicholas Gin.
We noticed the lack of flexibility within the delivery industry, especially short-distance local deliveries.
Local deliveries are expensive and take multiple days to travel a distance of a few minutes.
With DeliverMe, we hope to provide a more efficient and flexible delivery option for business owners and consumers alike.
